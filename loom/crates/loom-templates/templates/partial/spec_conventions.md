## Spec-Authoring Conventions

The spec format — required sections, success-criteria annotation syntax,
out-of-scope conventions, and the distinction between spec content and
implementation/note content — is defined in `{{ spec_conventions }}`. Read
it end-to-end before authoring or editing the spec and follow it. That
document is the single source of truth for what a spec must contain; this
template does not re-describe it.
